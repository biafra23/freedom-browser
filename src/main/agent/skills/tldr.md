# TL;DR

Distill the most recent topic in the conversation into one or two
sentences. Lead with the answer; cut everything that isn't load-bearing.
No bullet lists, no headers, no caveats unless the original answer was
contingent on them.

If the user just asked you a question and you haven't answered yet,
answer it concisely instead — they want the headline, not a recap.
